About CSI



Established in 1965, the Computer Society of India, or CSI is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It is a non-profit professional meet to exchange views and information learn and share ideas regarding advancement of theory and practice of Computer Engineering and Technology Systems, Science and Engineering, Information Processing and many more.


Objectives


To inspire and nurture students, and help them to integrate into the IT community. To give opportunity to students to interact with Industry professionals and chalk out a career path. To create awareness about trending topics in the IT field, by collaborating with distinguished academicians, researchers and practitioners. To encourage students to participate in various types of technical events like workshops, symposiums etc. To facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals.
